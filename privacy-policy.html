Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

WhisprMail is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or email content to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

All email processing happens locally on your device. Your Gmail credentials are stored securely using your operating system's credential manager.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communications with Gmail are encrypted using OAuth 2.0.

4. Third-Party Services

WhisprMail only connects to Gmail's official API. We do not share your data with any third parties.

5. Google Data Policy

WhisprMail complies with Google’s Limited Use requirements as described in the Google API Services User Data Policy.

6. Use of Google User Data for AI/ML

WhisprMail does not use any data obtained from Google APIs (including Gmail) to train, develop, or improve generalized AI or machine learning models.

7. Google User Data Access

WhisprMail requests access to specific Google user data through the Gmail API, including:

  • Your email address (for identifying the account in use)
  • Read access to incoming emails (to identify and summarize content)
  • Permission to mark emails as read or starred (to help users manage their inbox)
  • If applicable: Permission to delete emails upon user request

8. How We Use Google User Data

The data retrieved via Gmail API is used solely for the purpose of identifying new incoming emails, optimize and enhance experience and analyzing them locally on your device. This enables features like summarizing content or urgency detection. No email content, headers, or attachments are stored or sent to any external server.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

WhisprMail does not retain or permanently store any Google user data. All processing is performed in-memory and locally on the user's device. Once the app is closed or terminated, all data accessed during the session is discarded.
