Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

WhisprMail is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or email content to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

All email processing happens locally on your device. Your Gmail credentials are stored securely using your operating system's credential manager.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communications with Gmail are encrypted using OAuth 2.0.

4. Third-Party Services

WhisprMail only connects to Gmail's official API. We do not share your data with any third parties.
